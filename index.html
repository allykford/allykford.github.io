Stigmas Against VT Majors Podcast

Are you a college student who faces stigma about your major? Do you feel judged based on your standing as a student? For example, are you a transfer student, have you changed your major more than once, or are you an international student? No one likes to be put down or feel inferior, but it happens, especially when you walk the same halls as other students who study material far different than yours. In today's episode, we talk about the judgement we've faced as a diverse group of Virginia Tech students.
